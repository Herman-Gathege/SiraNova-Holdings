About Us

Siranova Holdings is a self-help investment group formed in January 2026 by a team of passionate individuals committed to financial empowerment and long-term economic growth.

The group was established to pool resources through member contributions and invest in sustainable income-generating projects. Our first major focus is maize milling and maize flour production, with the goal of supporting Kenya’s agricultural value chain.

Beyond business, Siranova promotes unity, entrepreneurship, and mutual support among its members while contributing to community development and economic opportunity.
