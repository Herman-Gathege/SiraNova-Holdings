Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

It depends on the size and complexity. Simple sites can take 1–2 weeks, while ecommerce projects may take 3–5 weeks.

Can I update my website myself?

Yes. We build on platforms that allow easy updates, and we can also train you on how to manage your site.

Do you provide domain and hosting?

We can help you purchase a domain and set up reliable hosting, or handle it all for you.

What does your pricing look like?

Our prices vary depending on the scope. We offer flexible packages to match your budget and goals.
